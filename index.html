🏙️ What is UrbanPulse?
A smart city intelligence dashboard aggregating real-time urban data to help citizens plan their daily lives smarter.

📌 What is an Urban Services Predictor?

An Urban Services Predictor is an AI-powered platform that collects, analyzes, and forecasts the status of essential city services — weather, transportation, utilities, and public facilities — for a specific geographic location.

It uses real-time data from government agencies, IoT sensors, satellite imagery, and historical patterns to predict urban conditions up to 48 hours ahead, empowering citizens to plan smarter every day.
